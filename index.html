Our Vision

The MustWorkout.com project aims to gather users from any place in the world, feeling like a part of a global community - sharing, discussing and rating the most valuable knowledge regarding strength sports, nutrition, health and all fit lifestyle.
We believe that creating such a place on the Internet will bring great benefits for the development of individuals, as well as will help in establishing new, international friendships between them, opening possibilities to travel and practice their common passion in the same time.
